Privacy Policy

App: Activity Tracker
Developer: Viral Systems
Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Activity Tracker ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

The App may collect the following information:

  • Account information – If you sign in with Google or Facebook, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from those providers.
  • Health & fitness data – Workout logs, diet entries, habit records, goals, and body stats that you enter manually.
  • Device information – Anonymous usage analytics to improve the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalise the App's features (dashboard, training, diet, habits, tasks, goals, AI coach).
  • To sync your data across devices via Firebase Cloud Firestore.
  • To generate personalised coaching advice within the App.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore and Firebase Authentication). Data is scoped to your individual user account and is not shared with other users. Firebase's security rules ensure only you can read or write your data.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase – Authentication and database
  • Google Sign-In – Optional sign-in method
  • Facebook Login – Optional sign-in method

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account exists. You can delete your data at any time by signing out and contacting us to request account deletion.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To make a request, contact us at the email below.
